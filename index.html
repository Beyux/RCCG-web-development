ABOUT US

The Rock Center Seattle (TRC Seattle) is a Parish of “The Redeemed Christian Church of God” (RCCG) - a global christian organization with its Headquarters in Nigeria. RCCG was established in 1957 by Pa Akindayomi and is currently led by the General Overseer worldwide - Pastor Enoch Adejare Adeboye. More on our history can be seen Here and more of our foundational beliefs can be seen here.

OUR MANDATE

In The Rock Center Seattle, our core value is to “build men, women and children of every race,
tribe and every tongue in the revelation of the knowledge of God.”

Our Culture

Our church culture includes the following

  1. Praying Church

    We believe that the Bible has called us to pray incessantly and in every situation.

  2. Loving Church

    We are here to provide a warm atmosphere, care and meet the needs of our church community.

  3. Word based church

    We are committed revealing the deep truth of the scripture, preaching the word of God in Season and encouraging one another.

  4. Aggressive Evangelism

    We believe that God has called us to preach the gospel unto all nations and we are therefore committed to turn Seattle around for Christ like Daniel, Shadrach, Meshach and Abednego.

  5. Giving Church

    we believe in giving out of love and to the building of God’s kingdom via outreaches.

Our Mission

Our mission in The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) includes the following:

  1. To make heaven.
  2. To take as many people with us.
  3. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.
  4. To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.
  5. To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  6. We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.
